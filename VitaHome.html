Privacy First

Your data never
leaves your iPhone.

In a world where health apps sell your most personal data, VitaTrack is different. Everything is stored locally using SwiftData — Apple's on-device persistence framework. The only external calls are food database lookups, and no health data is ever included.

✓
No account required

Jump straight in. No email, no password, no sign-up ever.

SwiftData on-device storage

All health data lives on your iPhone. Included in standard iCloud backups.

No third-party data sharing

Your data is never sold, shared, or sent anywhere. Period.
